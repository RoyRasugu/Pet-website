About Cats

with their adorable nature. It is no wonder most people
adopt them as pets. We all agree dog is the "so called"
man's friends but that is all in the past. Now with all
changing times, people are getting used to cats, taking
them to vaca, posting them on social media and all

the importance of hygiene and are very agile which really
captivates me alot.
